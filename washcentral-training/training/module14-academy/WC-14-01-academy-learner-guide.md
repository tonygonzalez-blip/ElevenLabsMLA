# WC-14-01 · Academy (LMS) — learner side (Academy)

v1.0 · July 9, 2026 · Author: Claude — Micrologic training project
**Verified against demo.washcentral.com on July 9, 2026.**

## Purpose

WashCentral ships with a built-in learning platform, the **Academy**. This lesson covers the learner side of it, starting from **My Dashboard**: the four progress tiles, the **Continue Learning** resume card, the **All Courses** grid, your **My Achievements** badges, and the **Org Leaderboard** and **Quick Links** panels. By the end you can read your own learning status at a glance, tell an enrolled course from an available one, and find the three other learner destinations in the sidebar. This is a browse-only tour. You will not enroll in or start a course.

## Audience and prerequisites

Internal Micrologic technicians using the Academy to skill up on WashCentral, and Pre-Configuration techs who will later guide client staff through it. You should be signed in. No prior Academy experience is assumed. The admin side of the Academy (the **ADMIN** sidebar group) is a separate lesson, WC-14-02. The session timeout ("Still there?") behavior is not part of this lesson; see WC-01-03.

## Navigation path

From the left rail, click the **Academy** icon (the graduation-cap glyph); the Academy workspace opens on **My Dashboard**. The account-menu route into the Academy is covered in WC-00-03. Inside the Academy the left sidebar carries the learner destinations under the **ACADEMY** heading: `Academy → My Dashboard / Course Catalog / Learning Paths / My Learning`.

## Steps

One action per step. Hold each new screen for two to three seconds before moving on. Nothing here is clicked that enrolls you or starts a course.

1. Open the Academy from the rail and land on **My Dashboard**. **Expected:** the sidebar header reads **WASHCENTRAL Academy**, the breadcrumb reads **Academy › My Dashboard**, and the page title is **My Dashboard** with the subtitle "Your learning overview — progress, assignments, and achievements." The global tools you already know carry over to the top-right: **Search ⌘K**, **Ask Shyne**, and your **MG** account avatar.
2. Read the **ACADEMY** sidebar group. **Expected:** four learner destinations are listed: **My Dashboard** (active now), **Course Catalog**, **Learning Paths**, and **My Learning**. Below them sits a second group headed **ADMIN** (**Admin Dashboard**, **Users & Enrollment**, **Course Management**, **Reports & Compliance**). The ADMIN group is the Academy's back office and is out of scope here; it is covered in WC-14-02.
3. Look at the four tiles across the top of the dashboard. **Expected:** the progress strip shows **Courses Enrolled**, **Completed**, **Hours Learned**, and **Certificates Earned**. On this demo account every tile reads **0** (Hours Learned reads **0h**), which is what a brand-new learner sees before any enrollment. These four numbers are your at-a-glance status.
4. Find the **Continue Learning** section and its **View All →** link. **Expected:** a single wide resume card sits under the tiles. This is where an in-progress course would surface so you can pick up where you left off, and **View All →** opens the fuller list. On a fresh account it previews the starter course rather than a course you have actually begun.
5. Read the **Continue Learning** card without clicking its buttons. **Expected:** the card carries two chips, **PLATFORM** and **BEGINNER**, the title **Getting Started with WashCentral**, a one-line course summary, a progress bar reading "0% complete · 0 of 6 lessons done", the runtime "45m total", and two buttons: **Start Course** and **Course Details**. Do not click **Start Course** in this lesson.
6. Move down to the **All Courses** section and its **Browse Catalog** button. **Expected:** a grid of course cards. Each card has a colored header with a category ribbon, then the course title, the instructor and duration on one line, a progress bar, a status line (**Not started**), a lessons-done count, and a **Start** button. **Browse Catalog** at the top-right of the section jumps to the full **Course Catalog**.
7. Read across the six course cards in the grid. **Expected:** the dashboard shows **Getting Started with WashCentral** (Rachel Kim · 45m · 0 of 6 lessons), **Command Center Mastery** (Rachel Kim · 1h 20m · 0 of 10), **Executive Dashboard & KPIs** (Dr. Priya Nair · 2h 15m · 0 of 13), **CRM Deep Dive: Customers & Members** (James Okafor · 1h 45m · 0 of 11), **Marketing Campaigns & Automation** (Sofia Reyes · 2h · 0 of 12), and **Sales & Transaction Management** (Tom Delgado · 1h 10m · 0 of 9). Each reads **Not started** here. Do not click any **Start** button.
8. Find the **My Achievements** section. **Expected:** the header reads "3 earned · 3 locked" and six badge tiles are shown: **Fast Starter**, **First Certificate**, **7-Day Streak**, **Analytics Pro**, **Top Learner**, and **Platform Expert**. Earned badges are filled in; locked ones are dimmed until you meet their condition.
9. Look at the **Org Leaderboard** panel on the right. **Expected:** a ranked list of colleagues by learning points, with the signed-in learner highlighted in the standings, and a **View Full Leaderboard →** link at the bottom. This is the Academy's team-engagement view; the ranking is view-only from here.
10. Below the leaderboard, find the **Quick Links** panel. **Expected:** a short list of dashboard shortcuts. The captured shortcuts include **My Learning Paths** and **My Certificates**, which jump straight to those views without hunting through the sidebar.
11. Point to the three remaining **ACADEMY** destinations in the sidebar: **Course Catalog**, **Learning Paths**, and **My Learning**. **Expected:** **Course Catalog** is the full course library (the dashboard's **Browse Catalog** button lands here), **Learning Paths** groups courses into sequenced tracks, and **My Learning** is your personal in-progress and completed list. The on-page layout of these three pages (catalog filters and sort, per-card rating and enrollment, the learning-path cards, and the My Learning list) is not captured in the July 9 verification corpus and is parked: TBD — parked (new item, see structured return). Author-to-operator note: capture these three pages live before recording.
12. Note the enrollment safety rule for this lesson. **Expected:** browsing the dashboard, hovering a card, or reading the catalog changes nothing. Enrolling in or starting a course is a real action, so in this lesson the **Start Course**, **Start**, and any **Enroll** / **Start Path** buttons are never clicked. Enrollment and taking a course are deferred to a later lesson.
13. Recap the learner side. **Expected:** **My Dashboard** is your home base. The four tiles show your status, **Continue Learning** resumes a course, **All Courses** and **Browse Catalog** reach the catalog, **My Achievements** and the **Org Leaderboard** track progress and standing, and the sidebar reaches **Course Catalog**, **Learning Paths**, and **My Learning** without enrolling in anything.

### Exercise 14.1a [READ-ONLY]

Knowledge check (reworded from the Module 14 orientation): On **My Dashboard**, where do you read (1) how many courses you have finished, (2) whether a given course has been started, and (3) which colleague leads the learning standings? Then name the two sidebar destinations you would open to (a) browse the full course library and (b) see your own in-progress courses. Answer without enrolling in anything. (Answers: (1) the **Completed** tile; (2) the **Not started** / lessons-done line on each **All Courses** card; (3) the top row of the **Org Leaderboard** panel. Library = **Course Catalog** (or **Browse Catalog**); your courses = **My Learning**.)

## Expected results

From the rail you open the **Academy** and land on **My Dashboard**. You can read your four progress tiles, resume from **Continue Learning**, scan the **All Courses** grid and tell an enrolled course from an available one, check your **My Achievements** badges, see where you sit on the **Org Leaderboard**, and reach **Course Catalog**, **Learning Paths**, and **My Learning** from the sidebar. Nothing you did enrolled you in a course or changed any record.

## Notes and troubleshooting (observed behavior only)

- The dashboard is read-only to browse. Reading tiles, cards, badges, and the leaderboard changes nothing. The only state-changing controls here are the enroll/start buttons (**Start Course**, **Start**), which this lesson does not click.
- The four tiles and every course card read **0** / **Not started** on this demo account because it has no enrollments yet. On a real learner account these fill in with actual progress.
- **Course Catalog**, **Learning Paths**, and **My Learning** are named and reachable from the sidebar, but their page contents were not captured in the July 9 corpus (the sweep returned a Not Found response for each). Their filters, card fields, and lists are parked until those pages are verified live; do not state their layout from memory or from the plan.
- The **ADMIN** sidebar group (**Admin Dashboard**, **Users & Enrollment**, **Course Management**, **Reports & Compliance**) belongs to the Academy admin side and is covered in WC-14-02.
- If the "Still there?" idle dialog appears during a recording, dismiss it with **Stay Logged In** off-camera and re-roll the segment. Session-timeout behavior is out of scope here; see WC-01-03.
