## Purpose

The WashCentral Academy has two halves. WC-14-01 covered the learner half. This lesson covers the **ADMIN** group in the Academy sidebar: the four pages where a Pre-Configuration technician enrolls client staff, manages the course library, and pulls completion and compliance reports. By the end you can read the **Admin Dashboard**'s training KPIs and completion heatmap, find a specific learner's required-course progress in **Users & Enrollment**, name the per-course levers in **Course Management**, and pull location- and course-level completion from **Reports & Compliance**. Everything is read: the enroll, edit, and announcement controls are located and named, never used.

## Audience and prerequisites

Internal Micrologic technicians, mainly Pre-Configuration and Onboarding staff who set up training for client crews. You should be signed in and comfortable in the Academy from WC-14-01. Enrolling other people, editing courses, and sending announcements are administrator actions; this lesson locates those controls and leaves them alone. **[READ-ONLY]**

## Navigation path

From the left rail, open **Academy**. Inside, the left sidebar carries two groups: **ACADEMY** (learner pages) and, below it, **ADMIN** with four destinations: **Admin Dashboard**, **Users & Enrollment**, **Course Management**, and **Reports & Compliance**. Reach all four by their sidebar links. Do not type their URLs: `lms-admin.html`, `lms-admin-users.html`, `lms-admin-courses.html`, and `lms-admin-reports.html` all answered HTTP 404 on the July 9 direct-URL sweep, while the sidebar links render every page.

## Steps

One action per step. Hold each new screen for two to three seconds before moving on. Nothing here enrolls, edits, archives, or sends.

1. From the rail, click **Academy**. **Expected:** the Academy loads on your dashboard. The breadcrumb reads **Academy › My Dashboard**, the brand shows **WASHCENTRAL Academy**, and the left sidebar carries the Academy's own navigation.
2. Read the two sidebar groups. **Expected:** the **ACADEMY** group holds the learner pages (**My Dashboard**, **Course Catalog**, **Learning Paths**, **My Learning**); the **ADMIN** group beneath it lists **Admin Dashboard**, **Users & Enrollment**, **Course Management**, and **Reports & Compliance**. The learning plan calls this group "LMS Administration"; the sidebar renders **ADMIN**, and the screen wins (see Notes).
3. Click **Admin Dashboard**. **Expected:** the page opens with the breadcrumb **Academy › Admin Dashboard** and the subtitle "Manage training across your organization." An **Enroll Users** button sits in the header — locate it, do not click it.
4. Read the five KPI tiles. **Expected:** **19 Active Learners** (Last 30 days), **26% Completion Rate** (All enrollments), **49 Overdue** (flagged **Action needed**, across all courses), **186 Total Enrollments** (Active records), and **9h 46m Avg Time Spent** (Per learner). The **Overdue** tile is the one a rollout conversation starts from.
5. Read the **Course Completion Heatmap** ("All 19 courses across 25 users"). **Expected:** a per-course breakdown with columns **COURSE · COMPLETION BREAKDOWN · ENROLLED · COMPLETE %**. The top row reads **Getting Started with WashCentral — 6 completed · 7 in progress · 6 overdue · 6 not started — 25 — 24%**. One glance per course tells you where a client crew's training actually stands.
6. Locate the admin actions on this page without using them. **Expected:** **Enroll Users** (header), **Manage**, and **New Announcement**. The July 11 capture records the enrollment form's fields (**Select Users**, **Enroll In**, **Course**, **Due Date (optional)**) and the announcement form's (**Title**, **Message**, **Audience**, with **Cancel** / **Send Announcement**). Every one of these creates real training records or sends a message; on the shared demo tenant none of them is opened for use. **[READ-ONLY]**
7. Click **Users & Enrollment**. **Expected:** breadcrumb **Academy › Users & Enrollment**, subtitle "Manage user enrollments, track progress, and assign courses." Four tiles: **TOTAL USERS 25** (Active learners), **AVG COMPLETION RATE 27%** (Required courses), **USERS WITH OVERDUE 17** (Need attention), **FULLY COMPLIANT 0** (All required completed). Toolbar: **Filters**, **Auto-Enroll Rules**, **Export CSV**, **Bulk Enroll**, and a **Search users by name, email, location...** box. Leave **Bulk Enroll**, **Auto-Enroll Rules**, and **Export CSV** alone.
8. Read the user table. **Expected:** columns **USER · LOCATION · REQUIRED PROGRESS · STATUS · LAST ACTIVITY · AVG SCORE · ACTIONS** (sortable columns carry a ↕). The first row reads **Marcus Johnson · m.johnson@demo.wc · General Manager · Fort Lauderdale · 3/7 · Overdue · 1 month ago · 78%**, with row actions **View Profile** and **Assign Course**. **REQUIRED PROGRESS** (like **3/7**) against **STATUS** (**Overdue** / **In Progress**) is the per-person compliance read; role chips on the users include **General Manager**, **Shift Supervisor**, **Cashier**, and **Regional Manager**. Do not click **Assign Course**.
9. Click **Course Management**. **Expected:** breadcrumb **Academy › Course Management**, subtitle "Manage courses, prerequisites, learning paths, and certifications," with three sections — **Courses**, **Learning Paths**, **Certifications** — and a toolbar of **Filters**, **New Course**, and **Import**. Neither **New Course** nor **Import** is clicked.
10. Read one course card. **Expected:** each course row carries its category and level ribbons, three stats — for **Getting Started with WashCentral**: **25 ENROLLED · 24% COMPLETE · 78 AVG SCORE** — an **On** publish toggle, and four controls: **Settings**, **Edit**, **Preview**, **Learners**. The **Settings** panel holds visibility options (**Visible to all users**, **Hidden**, **Required roles only**) with **Cancel** / **Save Settings**. Locate the toggle and controls; change nothing.
11. Click **Reports & Compliance**. **Expected:** breadcrumb **Academy › Reports & Compliance**, subtitle "Track completion, compliance, and training ROI across your organization." Seven report tabs run across the top: **Completion Overview**, **Learner Progress**, **Course Analytics**, **Compliance Status**, **Leaderboard**, **Time & Engagement**, **Scheduled Reports**, with **Schedule Report** and **Export CSV** buttons and a **Filters** / **Apply Filters** control. Four tiles: **Total Enrollments 186**, **Completion Rate 26%** (49 of 186 completed), **Overdue 49** (Require attention), **Avg Quiz Score 81%** (Across all graded items).
12. Read the two completion tables on **Completion Overview**. **Expected:** a **Completions Over Time** chart (Jun 2025 – May 2026), then **Completion by Location** (**LOCATION · USERS · ENROLLED · COMPLETED · COMPLETION % · OVERDUE · AVG SCORE**; top row **Fort Lauderdale · 5 · 44 · 12 · 27% · 13 · 81%**) and **Completion by Course** (**COURSE · ENROLLED · COMPLETED · COMPLETION % · AVG QUIZ SCORE · AVG TIME SPENT**; top row **Getting Started with WashCentral · 25 · 6 · 24% · 78% · 1h 59m**). This is the page the learning plan names for a client crew's completion and compliance numbers, and it is live.
13. Recap. **Expected:** four pages, four jobs. **Admin Dashboard** for the org-wide KPI and heatmap read, **Users & Enrollment** for the per-person compliance and the (unused) enroll controls, **Course Management** for the per-course levers, and **Reports & Compliance** for the location and course rollups a client review needs. All reached by sidebar, all read without changing a record.

**Knowledge check.** Where does a Pre-Config tech pull a client crew's course completion and compliance, and how do you reach it in this build? Answer: **Reports & Compliance** inside the Academy's **ADMIN** group — open it from the sidebar link (its direct URL 404'd on the July 9 sweep); the **Completion by Location** and **Completion by Course** tables carry the numbers, with **Export CSV** and **Schedule Report** as the admin-side outputs you locate but do not run in the demo.

You can reach all four **ADMIN** pages from the Academy sidebar and read each one: the **Admin Dashboard** tiles (**19 / 26% / 49 / 186 / 9h 46m**) and completion heatmap, the **Users & Enrollment** table with **REQUIRED PROGRESS** and **STATUS** per learner, the **Course Management** cards with their **On** toggles and **Settings / Edit / Preview / Learners** controls, and the **Reports & Compliance** tabs with completion by location and by course. ## Notes and troubleshooting (observed behavior only)

- **Sidebar, not URL.** All four admin pages answered HTTP 404 on their direct URLs in the July 9 sweep and rendered fully through the Academy sidebar on July 11. The v1.0 finding "not yet deployed" was wrong in its conclusion — the pages exist; the direct routes fail. If a client says an Academy admin page is "gone," ask how they opened it. (Reported in the July 11 return; supersedes the v1.0 parked item for the LMS admin interiors.)
- The July 9 capture of **Reports & Compliance** also hit a malformed host (`demo.washcentral.comml`) and never reached the server; the July 11 in-app capture resolves that open question — the page is real and complete.
- Naming discrepancy (carried from v1.0, still true): the learning plan calls the group "LMS ADMINISTRATION"; the sidebar renders **ADMIN**. The observed label wins.
- Cross-checks that hold on July 11: **186 Total Enrollments**, **26% Completion Rate**, and **49 Overdue** appear identically on the **Admin Dashboard** and **Reports & Compliance**; **25** users matches **Users & Enrollment**'s **TOTAL USERS** and the heatmap's "across 25 users."
- Read-only discipline: **Enroll Users**, **Bulk Enroll**, **Assign Course**, **Auto-Enroll Rules**, **New Course**, **Import**, **Edit**, **Archive**, the **On** toggles, **New Announcement** / **Send Announcement**, **Schedule Report**, and **Export CSV** all act on real training records or send messages. A technician documenting or training on these pages never enrolls real client staff, never edits or unpublishes a course, and never sends an announcement.
- If the "Still there?" idle dialog appears during recording, dismiss it off-camera with **Stay Logged In** and re-roll the segment (session behavior is WC-01-03's subject).
