# WC-01-01 — The Global Navigation Rail

v1.1 · July 8, 2026 · Author: Claude — Micrologic training project
**Verified against demo.washcentral.com on July 8, 2026.**

## Purpose

This lesson covers how to move around WashCentral. You will get to know the 13 operational modules and Settings on the global navigation rail, what each module contains, and how expanding the sidebar shows a module's section menu.

## Audience and prerequisites

Internal Micrologic technicians (all tracks). Prerequisites: WC-00-02 (Signing in) completed; you are signed in and on the **Command Center**. No special permission level observed for navigation.

## Navigation path

Global rail (dark vertical bar, far left, present on every screen), top to bottom.

## Steps

One action per step. Hold each new screen for two to three seconds.

1. On the **Command Center**, locate the dark rail on the far left. **Expected:** 14 icons top-to-bottom plus your avatar at the very bottom.
2. Click the **Expand sidebar** chevron near the bottom of the rail. **Expected:** the rail widens; header shows **WASHCENTRAL** with **● Command Center** as the current page; module labels become visible.
3. Click **Analytics**. **Expected:** **Executive Dashboard** loads ("The state of the business"); the sidebar lists section groups **ANALYTICS · MARKETING · INTELLIGENCE · FORECASTING** (22 pages).
4. Click **Marketing**. **Expected:** **Campaigns** loads; sidebar items include Campaigns, Automation, SmartLinks, Customer Segments, Offers & Promotions, Email/SMS/RCS Templates, Deliverability.
5. Click **CRM**. **Expected:** **Customers** loads with the KPI strip (Total Customers · Active Members · At Risk · Prospects); sidebar shows the CRM group plus **SERVICE DESK → Agent Console**.
6. Click **Sales**. **Expected:** **Tickets** loads (header group reads TRANSACTIONS); sidebar: Tickets, Ticket Details, Payments, Payouts, Shifts, Declined Credit Card Log, Gift Card Transactions, House Account Transactions.
7. Click **Inventory**. **Expected:** **Inventory Items** loads; sidebar: Items, Batches, Adjustments, Vendors, Chemicals & Supplies, Purchase Orders.
8. Click **Team**. **Expected:** **Employees** loads; sidebar groups **TEAM · SALES COACHING · GAMIFICATION**.
9. Click **Operations**. **Expected:** **Maintenance Issues** loads; sidebar groups **MAINTENANCE · INCIDENTS · SCHEDULING**.
10. Click **Digital**. **Expected:** **Portal Members** loads; sidebar groups **PORTAL · MOBILE APP**.
11. Click **IT & Support**. **Expected:** **Support Tickets** loads; sidebar includes **ML Command Center**, Help Desk (Zoho), Knowledge Base, System Alerts, Training Requests and the **AUDIT** group.
12. Click **Billing**. **Expected:** **Usage & Billing** loads (current billing period, SMS/Email usage).
13. Click **Academy**. **Expected:** **My Dashboard** loads (WashCentral Academy — courses, paths, certificates).
14. Click **Reports**. **Expected:** the Reports hub loads (VIEWS: All Reports 173 · Favorites · Scheduled Reports · Advanced Analytics · Custom Builder; 19 categories).
15. Click the **Settings** gear at the bottom of the rail. **Expected:** Settings home loads — "System configuration, setup, and platform preferences," counter **16 categories · 92 pages**.
16. In the Settings sidebar, click **← Close Settings**, then click **Command Center** on the rail. **Expected:** you are back on the Command Center. End of lesson.

## Expected results

You can reach any module in one click, name what lives in each, and use the expanded sidebar to read a module's section menu rather than guess where a section is.

## Notes and troubleshooting (observed behavior only)

- The rail is identical on every page; only the highlighted module changes.
- Each module page has its own secondary sidebar with grouped section links; read it before searching.
- Faster than clicking: **Ctrl+K** opens the command palette ("Search pages, modules, actions…"), covered in WC-01-02.
- If you learned WashAssist's 14-module map: Employees/Scheduler/Gamification are consolidated under **Team**; Maintenance under **Operations**; Mobile/Portal under **Digital**; Support/Audit under **IT & Support** (see DISCREPANCIES.md #2).
