# WC-02-02 · Customer 360 (CRM)

v1.1 · July 11, 2026 · Author: Claude — Micrologic training project
**Verified against demo.washcentral.com on July 11, 2026.**

## Purpose

Customer 360 is the single record you open for almost every member question: who they are, what plan they carry, what the POS scans, what they were charged, and what you have already said to them. This lesson walks the whole page in order, from the header band down through all nine tabs, so you can find any fact fast and know which controls are safe to touch. It runs in two parts. Part 1 covers the header band and the first five tabs. Part 2 covers the last four tabs, the **Actions ▾** menu, and a recap. You read everything here; you send nothing and you change nothing.

## Audience and prerequisites

Internal Micrologic technicians who support members. You should be signed in and able to reach **CRM → Customers**, which is where WC-02-01 (Customer List) leaves you. No prior CRM experience is assumed. Editing a customer and creating a new one are covered separately in WC-02-03.

## Navigation path

`CRM → Customers → [customer row] → Customer 360` (`crm-customer-detail.html`). The list lives in the left sidebar under **CRM → Customers**. Clicking a customer row opens that customer's 360 view; the page deep-links with a `?customer=` parameter, so a specific customer's view is shareable by URL.

**Grounding note (read once).** The Customer 360 view page (`crm-customer-detail.html`) was captured live and credentialed on July 11, 2026: the default view plus all nine tabs (digests `crm-customer-detail-overview` through `crm-customer-detail-notes`). The page anatomy below — header regions, metric band, Related chips, the nine tabs and their contents — is grounded in that capture. The July 11 record is a migrated member displaying as **Unknown** (**CUST-8888000000771774**); a different record shows its own values in the same layout. The one region the July 11 sweep did not land is the open **Actions ▾** menu (the capture attempt shows the menu closed), so its items remain `TBD — parked` (PARKED-ITEMS.md #4).

## Steps

One action per step. The same step numbers run through the shot list and the narration. Hold each new screen for two to three seconds before moving on. The example record used below is the member captured in the July 11 corpus, **Unknown** (**CUST-8888000000771774**), the first row of the Customer List; a live click on any row shows that member's own values in the same layout.

### Part 1 — Header band and tabs 1 to 5

1. From the **Customer List**, click a customer row. **Expected:** that customer's **Customer 360** view opens (`crm-customer-detail.html?customer=…`). A header band runs across the top, a six-tile metric band sits beneath it, a row of Related chips follows, and a tab bar begins the record body.
2. Read the header identity block on the left. **Expected:** an avatar with the member's initial and a green presence dot, the member's name, a plan badge (**No plan** on the July 11 record), and an identifier line of customer number · home location · account status — on the captured record it reads **CUST-8888000000771774 · — · Inactive** (the home location renders as a dash when none is set). The July 8 walk showed a risk-tier segment (for example "Low Risk") as a fourth part on its record. This is who you are looking at and where they belong.
3. Read the status chips under the identity line. **Expected:** four quick-status chips: **★ Unlimited Plus**, **Plate linked**, **Auto-pay active**, and **SMS subscribed**. They tell you at a glance that the plan is active, a plate is on file for the cameras, billing runs on auto-pay, and the member has opted in to text. **Caution (July 11):** on the captured record these four chips render even though the header reads **No plan** and **Inactive** — the chip row did not agree with the record's own status. Read the identifier line and the **Membership** tab before quoting a member's plan state; do not trust the chips alone (demo-data inconsistency, see notes).
4. Read the header buttons on the right. Do not click any of them. **Expected:** **← Back**, **Delete**, **Edit Customer**, and a green **Actions ▾** menu. **← Back** returns to the list, **Edit Customer** opens the edit form (WC-02-03), and **Actions ▾** holds the member-level actions you list in Part 2. **Delete** is never clicked in this lesson.
5. Read the metric band beneath the header. **Expected:** six tiles: **Member Since**, **Plan $/mo**, **Washes (30d)**, **Loyalty Points**, **Next Renewal**, and **Last Visit**. This tile set was confirmed on the view page in the July 11 capture, which settles the July 9 open question: the **Edit Customer** form's shared header shows **Lifetime Spend** and **Total Visits** in the second and third slots instead, and that difference is by design — the two pages carry different tile sets. On the July 11 record the tiles read — · — · 0 · 0 · — · Not available; a member with history shows real values.
6. Read the Related chips row (labelled **RELATED**). **Expected:** seven chips that jump to the member's related records: **Vehicles · Transactions · Wash Books · Gift Cards · House Account · Loyalty · Rewards**. Chips with related records carry a count badge — on the July 11 record, **Vehicles 1** and **Transactions 46**; the other five show no count on this record. Each chip is a shortcut into that slice of the account.
7. Click the **Overview** tab (the default tab). **Expected:** the **AI SUMMARY "Customer 360 Snapshot"** sits at the top with a **Regenerate** button. Below it: **Monthly Visits** ("Tunnel visits per month · last 12 months" — on the July 11 record **AVG / MONTH** 2.1, **PEAK MONTH** Apr '25, **LAST 30 DAYS** 3), a **Visit Breakdown** panel ("Service types across all visits" — Standard Wash 52%, Tire Shine 22%, Interior Vac 17%, Full Detail 9%, with "Add-on rate: 48%"), **Monthly Spend** ("Membership + add-ons · last 12 months" — **LIFETIME** $1,248, **AVG / MO** $68, **LAST MONTH** $79), and **Loyalty Points** (**TOTAL POINTS** 2,300, **TIER** Gold, **EXPIRES** Dec 2026). Do not click **Regenerate**; treat the summary as a draft you confirm against the tabs.
8. Click the **Contact** tab. **Expected:** a **Record Information** control at the top of the panel, then the member's record information in groups: **PERSONAL & BUSINESS** (FULL NAME, DATE OF BIRTH, GENDER, COMPANY, HOUSE ACCOUNT, SIGNATURE ON FILE), **ADDRESS** (STREET ADDRESS, LINE 2, CITY, STATE, ZIP CODE, COUNTRY), **PHONE & EMAIL** (PHONE, MOBILE, FAX, EMAIL ADDRESS), plus **NOTIFICATION PREFERENCES** (EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS, MONTHLY RECEIPTS, LAST PROMO SHOWN) and **MESSAGING CONSENT** (TRANSACTIONAL MESSAGING, PROMOTIONAL MESSAGING). This tab is read-only here; editing these fields is WC-02-03.
9. Click the **Membership** tab. **Expected:** four groups. **PLAN DETAILS** (MEMBERSHIP TYPE, HOME LOCATION, MEMBER SINCE, STATUS, MONTHS REMAINING, REWARD PROGRAM); **LOYALTY & POINTS** (LOYALTY CARD 1, LOYALTY CARD 2, **BARCODE**, TEMP BARCODE, ACCUMULATES POINTS, REWARDS EXPIRE); **VISIT & SPEND STATS** (FIRST VISIT, LAST VISIT, TOTAL VISITS, TOTAL POINTS, TOTAL AMOUNT SPENT); and **BILLING CYCLE** (RENEWAL DATE, LAST CHARGE, NEXT CHARGE, LAST PAYMENT RESULT). The **BARCODE** here is what the POS scans (on the July 11 record it reads **4LOMYBUT**, the same value as the vehicle plate on file), so this tab is your first stop for a "my tag won't scan" ticket, and **NEXT CHARGE** answers "when am I billed again."
10. Click the **Payments** tab. Do not add a card. **Expected:** **PAYMENT METHODS** with a **+ Add Card** control, and **PREPAID & CREDITS** listing any prepaid books on file (on the July 11 record it reads "No prepaid books on file for this customer."). You read the cards on file and any credit here; you do not add or edit a payment method in this lesson.
11. Click the **Vehicles** tab. Do not add a vehicle. **Expected:** **VEHICLES ON ACCOUNT** with a **+ Add Vehicle** control, and a card per vehicle showing the plate (July 11 record: **4LOMYBUT**), an **Open Vehicle →** link, **STATE / MAKE / MODEL** fields, and a **RETRACTS** list of the equipment holds applied to this account's washes (on the captured record: Tire Shine, Top Brush, Pick-up Top Blowers). The vehicles here are the plates the cameras match to the plan.

*Part 1 ends here. Part 2 continues on the same record without leaving the page.*

### Part 2 — Tabs 6 to 9, Actions menu, and recap

12. Click the **Linked Accounts** tab. Do not link anything. **Expected:** the **LINKED ACCOUNTS** panel, described on screen as "Additional accounts linked to this customer — family members, co-drivers, or shared memberships.", with a **+ Link Account** control. This is where shared plans and household relationships show up.
13. Click the **Activity** tab. **Expected:** **VISIT & TRANSACTION HISTORY**, the running log of what the member did and when. On a member with no wash history the panel reads "No wash visit history found for this customer." (the July 11 record's state); entries, when present, list newest first.
14. Click the **Messaging** tab. Do not type and do not send. **Expected:** the two-way text view, **"CRM · TWO-WAY MESSAGING — Text Conversation"**, showing the destination ("SMS to —" on the July 11 record, since no mobile is on file), the opt-in status ("SMS opt-in confirmed"), the thread (empty state: "No messages yet. Say hello below."), and a compose box with a **Send message** button. You read the history here to see what was already said. Sending is out of scope: never send a message from this tab in the demo.
15. Click the **Notes** tab. Do not save a note. **Expected:** **Customer Notes** ("Internal staff notes — not visible to customer") with a compose box ("Add a note about this customer…"), a **Pin to top** checkbox, and **Clear** and **Save Note** buttons (empty state: "No notes yet. Add the first one above."); below it, **Communication History** ("All channels, newest first") with channel filters **All / SMS / Email / Calls / Push / In-App**, and a **Change History** control. Notes are the internal record; the customer never sees them.
16. Click **Actions ▾** to open the menu, read the items, then close it without choosing one. **Expected:** the menu lists **Issue Wash** and **Refund Customer** (July 8 walk). The July 11 capture attempt for the open menu did not land — the shot shows the menu closed — so the current item list is still `TBD — parked (PARKED-ITEMS.md #4)`; confirm it on camera. The legacy guide describes six membership actions (including Cancel, Reset, and Pause Membership); only the two above have been observed live. Both are send- or money-type actions: never click **Issue Wash** or **Refund Customer** in the demo. Close the menu with **Esc** or by clicking **Actions ▾** again.
17. Recap the record. **Expected:** from one page you read who the member is (header and chips), the headline numbers (metric band), the account slices (Related chips), and nine tabs that answer the common tickets: the barcode the POS scans and the next charge date live on **Membership**, consent lives on **Contact** and **Messaging**, and the internal trail lives on **Notes**. You send nothing and change nothing to answer any of it.

## Expected results

From a single customer row you open a complete member record and read it end to end without changing anything. You can name the plan and status from the header, read the six headline numbers from the metric band, jump to a related record from the Related chips, and work all nine tabs: the AI snapshot and charts on **Overview**, the contact and consent fields on **Contact**, the scannable **BARCODE** and **NEXT CHARGE** on **Membership**, the cards and prepaid credit on **Payments**, the plates and equipment retracts on **Vehicles**, the household links on **Linked Accounts**, the visit log on **Activity**, the SMS thread on **Messaging**, and the internal notes and change trail on **Notes**. You list the **Actions ▾** items without running them.

## Notes and troubleshooting (observed behavior only)

- The Customer 360 view page and all nine tabs were captured live and credentialed on July 11, 2026 (digests `crm-customer-detail-*`). Every region, group, and field list in this guide traces to that capture except the open **Actions ▾** menu (below) and the risk-tier segment noted at step 2 (July 8 walk).
- **Metric band resolved (July 11).** The view page's six tiles are confirmed as **Member Since · Plan $/mo · Washes (30d) · Loyalty Points · Next Renewal · Last Visit**. The **Edit Customer** form's shared header shows **Lifetime Spend** and **Total Visits** in the second and third slots instead; the two pages differ by design. Do not carry the edit-form tile labels onto the view.
- The **Edit Customer** form tabs differ from the Customer 360 view tabs. The view has nine tabs (Overview, Contact, Membership, Payments, Vehicles, Linked Accounts, Activity, Messaging, Notes). The July 9 edit form shows **Contact, Membership, Payments, Vehicles, Communication, Comments, Record Information** instead. This is expected because the edit form is a different page; do not carry edit-form tab names onto the view.
- On July 11 the view page loaded the record matching the row clicked (the list's first row, **Unknown / CUST-8888000000771774**), so the fixed-sample-record behavior seen on some demo edit pages was not observed on the view. Still read the identifier line to confirm which member you are on.
- **Demo-data inconsistencies on the July 11 record (read before quoting values to anyone).** The header reads **No plan · Inactive** while the chip row shows **★ Unlimited Plus / Plate linked / Auto-pay active / SMS subscribed**; and the Overview panels show visit, spend, and points figures (2.1 avg visits/month, $1,248 lifetime, 2,300 points) while the metric band and the **Membership** tab's **VISIT & SPEND STATS** on the same record read 0 / $0.00. Treat the **Membership** tab fields as the record of truth and the chips and Overview panels as summaries that can lag or disagree in the demo tenant.
- **Actions ▾**: the July 8 walk listed **Issue Wash** and **Refund Customer** only (see `DISCREPANCIES.md #6`); the July 11 open-menu capture attempt missed (menu closed in the shot). The item list stays parked at `PARKED-ITEMS.md #4` pending a capture that shows the menu open.
- **Platform drift (July 11):** the CRM sidebar now carries two items that were not in the July 9 capture, **Contract Renewals** (after House Accounts) and **Prepaid Activity** (after Prepaid Books). They do not change this lesson's steps; they are noted for the curriculum map and DISCREPANCIES.md.
- Nothing on this page is sent or changed by reading it. The send-type and money-type controls are **Issue Wash**, **Refund Customer**, the Messaging compose box, and the Payments and Vehicles add controls. None of them are clicked in this lesson.

**Knowledge check.** Which tab and field do you open to verify (1) the barcode the POS scans, (2) the member's next charge date, (3) SMS consent, and (4) which vehicles auto-activate the plan? (Answers: 1 and 2 are the **BARCODE** and **NEXT CHARGE** fields on the **Membership** tab; 3 is the opt-in status on **Messaging**, backed by **MESSAGING CONSENT** on **Contact**; 4 is the **Vehicles** tab.)
