# WC-00-03 · The Account Menu and Personal Setup (Setup)

v1.0 · July 9, 2026 · Author: Claude — Micrologic training project
**Verified against demo.washcentral.com on July 9, 2026.**

## Purpose

Find the account menu, know what each item does, and change the two personal settings you will actually touch: Dark Mode and your training. The account menu hangs off your avatar in the top-right corner of every page, so it travels with you the same way Search and the location scope do.

## Audience and prerequisites

Internal Micrologic technicians new to WashCentral. You should be signed in and sitting on the Command Center, which is where WC-00-02 leaves you. No prior experience assumed.

## Navigation path

`Any page → avatar (top-right, your initials) → account menu`. The avatar shows your initials, not a photo. On the demo tenant it reads **MG**.

## Steps

One action per step. Hold each new screen for two to three seconds.

1. Look at the top-right of the header and find your avatar. **Expected:** a round badge showing your initials sits at the far right of the header, past **Search ⌘K**, **All Locations▾**, and **Ask Shyne**. On the demo it reads **MG**.
2. Click the avatar. **Expected:** the account menu opens. Its header shows your display name and your tenant code, so you can confirm at a glance which company and which account you are signed into.
3. Read the menu items. **Expected:** the menu lists **Profile**, **My Training**, **Notifications**, **Preferences**, a **Dark Mode** toggle, **Help Center**, and **Logout**. Profile and Preferences are where your personal account settings live. Notifications and Help Center are self-explanatory. My Training and Dark Mode are the two you will use most, and they are next.
4. Click the **Dark Mode** toggle. **Expected:** the interface switches to the dark theme immediately. This is a personal display preference. It changes how the platform looks for you only and touches no data.
5. Click **Dark Mode** again to switch back. **Expected:** the interface returns to the light theme. Leave it wherever you prefer to work; nothing about the toggle affects other users.
6. Open the account menu again and click **My Training**. **Expected:** the WashCentral Academy opens at `lms-home.html`. This is the learning platform you use throughout this program, so it is worth knowing the account menu is the fast way back to it from anywhere.
7. Return to your work. **Expected:** navigate back to the **Command Center** (or any working page). Do not click **Logout** unless you actually mean to sign out; it ends your session and returns you to the sign-in page.

## Expected results

You can open the account menu from any page, confirm which account and tenant you are in, flip Dark Mode to suit how you like to work, and jump to your Academy training in one step. None of these change platform data.

## Notes and troubleshooting

- The avatar is initials, not a photo, and it doubles as the "which account am I in" check. If the tenant code in the menu header is not the one you expect, you may be signed into the wrong tenant. See WC-00-02 for the three-field sign-in and the tenant code.
- Dark Mode is per-user and view-only. It is safe to toggle mid-shift.
- **My Training** is the same Academy you reach from the LMS lessons in Module 14. The account menu is just a shortcut to it.
- **Logout** ends your session. If you land back on the sign-in page unexpectedly, check whether Logout was clicked or whether an idle timeout fired (the "Still there?" dialog and session timeouts are covered in WC-01-03).
